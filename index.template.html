Important Notice: PepeBlocks Wallet is currently in BETA. Please backup your private key immediately.

We highly recommend importing your private key into the official wallet to ensure uninterrupted access to your funds in case this website becomes unavailable.

To import your private key into the Pepecoin Core Wallet and backup your wallet.dat file, follow these steps:

  1. Download and install Pepecoin Core Wallet from the official website.
  2. Launch Pepecoin Core Wallet and wait for it to synchronize with the network.
  3. Go to the 'File' menu and select 'Import Private Key'.
  4. Enter your private key and optionally a label, then click 'Import'.
  5. After importing your private key, backup your wallet.dat file by going to 'File' > 'Backup Wallet' and save it in a secure location.

Confirmation Required
  • Please confirm that you understand the following:

  • • There's no way to recover your funds if you lose access to your private key.
  • • It's highly recommended to back up your private key and import it into the official Pepecoin Core Wallet using the provided instructions.
  • • Before transferring larger amounts, test the process by sending small amounts to your receiving address and verifying they are received on both wallets.
  • • Never share your private key with anyone. If someone obtains it, they can access and steal your funds.
  • • We are not responsible for any loss of funds due to failure to back up your private key or import it into the official wallet.
